Recipe for a sourdough bread

Prepare your levain:
- mix sourdough starter, flour and water
- wait until the levain is active and bubbly

Prepare your bread:
- mix levain, flour, water and salt in a bowl
- cover and let sit for 4 hours
- during these 4 hours, perform a set of stretch and folds one hour apart
- preshape and shape your dough
- cold ferment in the fridge for 12h to 24h
- bake in a Remoska for 45 minutes
- in the last 10 minutes, cover with tinfoil to prevent burning
